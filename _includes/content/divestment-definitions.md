## Divestment categories

For each category of ecocidal industry/company we have listed in [our demands](/university-divestment), each college will fit into one of the following criteria. For example, a college can be fully divested from fossil fuels but non-divested from arms. To meet the demands fully, the college must be fully divested from all industries/companies listed.

**Non-divested**

In the given ecocidal industry/company, the college:
* Holds direct investments
* Has no commitments to partially or fully divest, or to never hold investments again

**Committed to partially divest**

The college is non-divested, but has committed to fulfil the criteria of partial divestment by a certain date.

**Partially divested**

In the given ecocidal industry/company, the college:
* Holds no direct investments (i.e. only holds indirect investments)
* Has committed to never hold direct investments again

**Committed to fully divest**

The college is non-divested or partially divested, but has committed to fulfil the criteria of full divestment by a certain date.

**Fully divested**

In the given ecocidal industry/company, the college:
* Holds no direct or indirect investments
* Has committed to never hold direct or indirect investments again

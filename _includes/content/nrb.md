### WILL I BE AT RISK OF ARREST?

The Northern Rebellion is a low risk event. There will be stewards and volunteers to manage the space.
Rebels who camp in the space are at a higher risk although we will be carrying out full police liaison in advance.

No one will be under pressure to take ‚Äòarrestable‚Äô action, and there will be full days of talks, training, and meeting other rebels for those that do not wish to take part in higher risk forms of civil disobedience.

All direct action comes with some risk of arrest so we advise you to read the [Legals section of XR‚Äôs website](https://rebellion.earth/act-now/resources/legal-info/), if you want to understand more.

**Advance Training:**

There is a workshop on ‚ÄòPolice Tactics and How to Stay Safe‚Äô being held in Manchester on August 11th (aimed at rebels wanting to take more risky action at future actions, or just those interested to know more).

There is training for people who would like to become Legal Observers, in central Manchester on August 18th.
(No experience necessary, legal observers act as neutral parties during an action and record what is done and said).

### WHY IS IT IN MANCHESTER?

Climate change is affecting us all. And the whole of the UK will need to change its approach if we are to achieve net carbon neutrality by 2025. Northern councils control everything from local transport and roads to council pension funds. Local MPs also have a significant voice in Westminster. The Northern Rebellion is a chance for them to see that their constituents are serious about the need for urgent change and are calling on them to act now.

### WHAT ABOUT THE INDIVIDUALS THAT WILL BE DISRUPTED?

We would not disrupt members of the public if it were not absolutely necessary and we acknowledge the inconvenience we may cause. The UN‚Äôs Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change says that we have less that 12 years to make drastic changes, if we are to limit climate catastrophe. We believe that non-violent direct action is the only way to persuade the government to take the absolutely urgent action required.

### WHAT ABOUT EMERGENCY VEHICLES?

We will be working with police in coming weeks to ensure that alternative routes are in place so any emergency vehicles can get through.
